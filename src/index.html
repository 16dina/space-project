What is space?

Space is the infinite black area in the universe beyond the top of the Earth’s atmosphere. Space starts at 100 kilometres above the sea level of Earth. If you look at the sky in the night you can see stars and the Moon. If you’re lucky you can see a planet. There is no air in space, that’s why it appears to be black. There is no gravity in space, so objects in space are floating.
